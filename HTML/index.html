"Got to Be There" is the debut solo single by the American singer Michael Jackson, written by Elliot Willensky and released as a single on October 7, 1971, on Motown Records. The song was produced by Hal Davis and recorded at Motown's Hitsville West studios in Hollywood.Known as the "King of Pop", he is regarded as one of the most significant cultural figures of the 20th century. The eighth child of the Jackson family, Jackson made his public debut in 1964 with his older brothers.
